About Trackflix:

Trackflix is a web application capable of recommending movies based on your music interests. Ever wonder what movies your favorite band has performed in? What about movies similar to those? With the input of a band or musical artist's name, Trackflix finds up to ten movies in the IMDB database featuring that artist's music, as well as the top six most similar movies to each result, calculated using tf*idf and cosine similarity scores of the plots of those movies.
